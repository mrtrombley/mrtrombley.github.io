About Me



A brief overview of the life of Matthew Trombley.

Who I Am

    Hello! My name is Matthew Trombley, and thanks for stopping by my website. I am currently a graduate student at NC State University in Raleigh, North Carolina. As a student at Clemson, I am pursuing a B.S. in Mathematical Sciences with a concentration in Operations Research & Management Science, and an Engineering minor. I originally started out majoring in mechanical engineering before switching over in the Spring of 2016, so I have some experience in engineering fields (ex. statics, dynamics, mechanics of materials, engineering graphics, and electrical engineering). I hope to steer my career path towards analytics or data science so I'm working on my degree in statistics while self-teaching coding skills on the side. In the next few years I am planning on teaching myself languages including Python, Pig, and Pandas, as well as improving my R and SAS skills. Some of the classes I'll be taking in the coming years are:

  • Advanced Mathematical Modeling
  • Systems Modeling
  • Regression and Time-Series Analysis
  • Production Planning & Control
  • Introduction to Stochastic Modeling

     I have already taken classes on statistical programming and theory of probability. I'm planning on doing some analytics projects which will be posted on my site in the coming months. If you're interested in those check out the "Posts" page in the navigation bar above.
    Here at Clemson, I'm Vice President of the IPTAY Student Advisory Board (ISAB). This board serves as the liaison between the Clemson student body and IPTAY, Clemson's athletic booster organization. This organization is one of the largest in the nation. As Vice President, I oversee the new member application process as well as help manage day-to-day activities of the board. The Student Advisory Board promotes many sports and student-athletes on campus, as well as runs events to benefit student IPTAY members.
