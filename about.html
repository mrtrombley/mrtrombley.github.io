About Me



A brief overview of the life of Matthew (Matt) Trombley.

Who I Am

    Hello! My name is Matt Trombley, and thanks for stopping by my website. I am currently a graduate student at NC State University in Raleigh, North Carolina. I am pursuing an M.S. in Analytics at the Institute for Advanced Analytics. At Clemson, I served as the Vice President of the IPTAY Student Advisory Board (ISAB). This board serves as the liaison between the Clemson student body and IPTAY, Clemson's athletic booster organization. This organization is one of the largest in the nation. As Vice President, I oversaw the new member application process as well as helped manage day-to-day activities of the board. The Student Advisory Board promotes many sports and student-athletes on campus, as well as runs events to benefit student IPTAY members.

In my spare time, I enjoy
  • Working on my Jeep
  • Golfing
  • Pick-up sports
  • Video Capture, Editing, and Production
  • Video games, including Fortnite, Madden, and Call of Duty
  • Working with broken electronics
  • Traveling (some of my favorite locations are Hawaii and California)

    Besides the above, I enjoy being outdoors and hanging out with the friends I've made here at Clemson and NC State. Whether it's hiking in the Blue Ridge Mountains or playing on intramural sports teams, I thrive off being active. And, you'll always catch me wearing orange on Fridays.
    Growing up I always enjoyed being entrepreneurial, whether it was buying broken electronics, fixing, and reselling them or selling my graphics services. I have always enjoyed creating graphics and videos through the Adobe Creative Suite. After Effects has been my program of choice for years. All of my skills in the Creative Suite have been self-taught. Being able to use these creative tools to turn an idea into a visual product has been a passion of mine. I hope to be able to put these skills to use one day in combination with my analytics skills to create data visualizations.
